Executive Summary

Service Delivery Model

PHI Central provides comprehensive administrative infrastructure through fiscal sponsorship, enabling programs to focus on mission delivery while Central handles operational complexity.

Key Value Proposition:
  • Full administrative infrastructure without standalone setup costs
  • Compliance management across federal, state, and local requirements
  • Economies of scale through shared services model
  • Risk mitigation through established systems and expertise
  • Focus on program outcomes rather than administrative burden
Service Funding Structure:
  • Primary: Two-tier IDC rate on all program funding
  • Additional: Award management fees for high-complexity programs
  • Direct: Actual cost pass-through for program-specific benefits

Funding Structure

Indirect Cost (IDC) Rate
  • Two-tier structure for cost recovery
  • First tier: Applied to modified total direct costs
  • Second tier: Applied to subcontract, subaward, and grant costs
  • IDC revenue funds PHI Central services
  • Rate subject to annual federal negotiation
Additional Fees
  • Award management fees for services beyond IDC coverage
  • Fringe benefits charged separately (actual cost basis)
  • Group policies maintained centrally by PHI
